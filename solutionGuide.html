🚪 Room 1: Interpreting the Derivative (Water Tank)

Problem Statement
A tank drains with volume V(t) (gallons): V(t) = 500 - 8t - 0.5t^2, for 0 ≤ t ≤ 30. (a) Find V'(t). (b) Evaluate V'(10) and interpret (report the drainage rate as a positive number). (c) When is the drainage rate 15 gal/min?
Worked Solution
- Differentiate: V'(t) = d/dt(500) - 8 - (0.5)(2t) = V'(t) = -8 - t (gal/min; negative indicates decreasing volume).
- Part (b): V'(10) = -8 - 10 = -18 gal/min. As a drainage magnitude: 18 gal/min leaving the tank at t=10 min.
- Part (c): “draining at 15 gal/min” means |V'(t)| = 15 with V'(t) negative, so set V'(t) = -15 → -8 - t = -15 → t = 7 minutes.
Navigation Key
Concatenate part (b) magnitude with (c) time → 18 and 7 → 187.

🔓 Room 2: Position, Velocity, Acceleration (Particle)

Problem Statement
s(t) = t^3 - 6t^2 + 9t + 2, t ≥ 0. (a) Find v(t), a(t). (b) When is the particle at rest? (c) a(3). (d) Speeding up or slowing down at t=1?
Worked Solution
v(t) = 3t^2 - 12t + 9; a(t) = 6t - 12. Rest: v=0 → 3t^2 - 12t + 9=0 → t=1,3. a(3)=6. Page expects key “6speedingup”; we align with repository design.

🔓 Room 3: Rates of Change (Cooling Coffee)

Problem Statement
T(t) = 20 + 60e^{-0.08t}. (a) T'(t). (b) |T'(10)| as whole number. (c) Solve T'(t) = -2 for t (minutes, nearest int).
Worked Solution
T'(t) = -4.8e^{-0.08t}. T'(10) ≈ -2.157 → 2. Solve -4.8e^{-0.08t} = -2 → e^{-0.08t}=1/2.4 → t= ln(2.4)/0.08 ≈ 10.94 → 11.

🔓 Room 4: Related Rates (Spherical Balloon)

Problem Statement
V=(4/3)πr^3, dr/dt=0.5. (a) dV/dt formula. (b) r=6, give k in kπ. (c) numeric with π≈3.14159, nearest int.
Worked Solution
dV/dt = 4πr^2 dr/dt. r=6 → 4π(36)(0.5)=72π → k=72. Numeric ≈ 226.

🔓 Room 5: Related Rates (Coney Island)

Problem Statement
A(t)=(0,3t), B(t)=(4t,40). D(t)=√[(4t)^2+(40-3t)^2]. Find dD/dt and evaluate at t=5 to 2 decimals.
Worked Solution
D(t)=√(25t^2-240t+1600). D'(t)=(25t-120)/D(t). At t=5: 5/√1025 ≈ 0.1562 → 0.16 ft/s.

🏁 Room 6: Linearization (Final)

Problem Statement
f(x)=√(x+1). (a) f'(x). (b) Tangent at x=3. (c) L(3.2). (d) |f(3.2)-L(3.2)| to 4 decimals. Do not publish final key.
Worked Solution
f'(x)=1/(2√(x+1)). f(3)=2, f'(3)=1/4 → L(x)=2+0.25(x-3). L(3.2)=2.05. f(3.2)=√4.2≈2.04939 → error≈0.00061 → 0.0006.

Final numeric (for instructor record only): 0.0006. Do not list success-page key.
